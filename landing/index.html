How It Works: 4 Simple Steps

  1. Download the App: Get the official Quality Connect app using the download button below. This app is where you'll manage your participation.
  2. Generate QR Code: Inside the app, select the product set to generate your unique, one-time QR code.
  3. Claim at Partner Store: Visit a participating store (Watsons, SM Beauty/LOOK, Beauty Bar) and present your QR code to claim your free kit.
  4. Submit Your Review: After trying the products, record a simple, honest video review (30-60 seconds) and submit it through the app.
